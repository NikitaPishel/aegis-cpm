### Internal defintions
$$
a = \frac{x}{L}
$$

### Distributed force
$$
F_d(x) = F_i \sin(\pi a)
$$

### Shear force:
$$
F_b(x) = \int F_d(x)\, dx
$$
$$
F_s(x) = \frac{FL}{\pi} \cos(\frac{\pi x}{L})
$$

### Bending moment:
$$
F_b(x) = \int\int F_d(x)\, dx
$$
$$
F_b(x) = \int F_s(x)\, dx
$$
$$
F_b(x) = \frac{FL^2}{\pi^2} \cos(\frac{\pi x}{L})
$$

### Force on joints:
$$
F_{j1}(x) = F(1-a)
$$
$$
F_{j2}(x) = Fa
$$
